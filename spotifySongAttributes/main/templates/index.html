Discover Your Music Taste Profile

Welcome to Genre Distro, an intelligent music analysis tool that helps you visualize and explore your unique listening preferences. Our app securely connects to your Spotify account to analyze your top tracks and create personalized genre distribution charts.

When you log in with Spotify, we only request access to read your top tracks and playlists. Your listening data is analyzed to identify genre patterns, allowing us to generate custom playlists that either match your current taste or help you discover new music. All processing happens during your session - we don't permanently store your listening history or personal information.

You can also analyze any public Spotify playlist to see its genre breakdown and compare it with your own taste profile. This is perfect for discovering how your music preferences align with friends or favorite curators.

This project was developed at the University of Cincinnati. For questions or feedback, please contact Chris Laney at Laneyct@mail.uc.edu.
